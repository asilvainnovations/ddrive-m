Welcome & Getting Started
What is DDRiVE-M · Who it's for · System requirements
🛡
What is DDRiVE-M?
DDRiVE-M stands for Disaster Risk Reduction & Management Intelligence Value Engine - Metro. It's your command center for tracking hazards, managing risks, responding to incidents, and building resilience across the Philippines. Whether you're at NDRRMC headquarters, an LGU disaster office, or monitoring remotely — DDRiVE-M keeps you informed and ready.

Before You Begin: Requirements & Account Setup
System requirements and getting your account
▾

System Requirements

  • Internet: Stable connection required for real-time data sync
  • Browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (modern versions)
  • Device: Laptop/desktop preferred; mobile fully supported
  • Account: Registered account with permission level assigned

Getting Your Account

  1. Contact your organization's DDRiVE-M administrator
  2. Provide your name, position, and organization
  3. Wait for welcome email with login credentials
  4. Use initial password to log in on first access
  5. Change password immediately after first login

▾

A visual 5×5 chart showing where your risks fall based on Likelihood (how often will this happen, scale 1–5) and Impact (how bad will consequences be, scale 1–5). Multiply Likelihood × Impact = Risk Score.

AI Vulnerability Scanner & Risk Registry
Machine learning analysis and risk management table
▾
◈
AI Vulnerability Scanner
Machine learning tool trained on Philippine disaster history. Analyzes your registry for patterns across five domains: Physical Infrastructure, Environmental Conditions, Operational Continuity, Cyber/Digital Systems, and Organizational Capacity. Lower percentage = higher vulnerability = focus improvement efforts there.

Adding a New Risk

  1. Click "+ Add Risk" button in the Risk Registry section
  2. Fill form: Risk Title (required), Category (dropdown), Likelihood score (1–5), Impact score (1–5), Owner department
  3. System calculates score automatically (L × I)
  4. Click "Register Risk" — confirmation toast appears, entry shows in table immediately

DDRiVER AI Assistant
Intelligent expert guidance on Philippine DRRM laws and protocols
◈
What Is DDRiVER AI?
DDRiVER is an intelligent conversational assistant providing expert guidance on Philippine disaster risk reduction laws, international standards (ISO 31000, ISO 22301, UNDRR), and emergency protocols. Think of it as a subject matter expert available 24/7 for quick fact-checking and procedural references.

Account Settings
Profile, general, security, notifications, and AI preferences
⚙️
Five Settings Sections Overview
Profile · General · Security · Notifications · AI Assistant
▾
👤
Profile Settings
  • Full Name: Min 2 characters
  • Role: Super Admin, Field Operator, Analyst, etc.
  • Organization: Max 100 characters
  • Email: Valid format required
  • Photo Upload: JPG/PNG under 5MB
🌐
General Settings
  • Theme: Light / Dark (auto-detect default)
  • Language: English PH, Filipino, Cebuano
  • Timezone: PST (UTC+8) default
  • Offline Mode: Enable/disable checkbox
🔒
Security Settings
  • Password minimum 8 characters with uppercase/lowercase/numbers
  • Cannot reuse last 3 passwords
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) enable/disable
  • Active sessions list — terminate suspicious remote access
🔔
Notification Settings
  • Email: On by default, unlimited daily
  • SMS: Off by default, max 5/day
  • Push: On, max 50/day
  • Priority filter: All / High+Critical / Critical only
  • Quiet Hours: suppress non-critical during set window
