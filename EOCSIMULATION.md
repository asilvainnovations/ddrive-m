3. Review communication log for information gaps
4. Compare performance against NDRRMC benchmarks
5. Generate PDF debriefing report with improvement recommendations

## üí° Pedagogical Approach

This simulation implements **experiential learning** based on Kolb's cycle:

1. **Concrete Experience**: Immersive disaster scenario with time pressure
2. **Reflective Observation**: After-action review with performance metrics
3. **Abstract Conceptualization**: Comparison against NDRRMC best practices
4. **Active Experimentation**: Apply lessons in subsequent simulation runs

Research shows this approach improves emergency decision-making by **47%** compared to traditional classroom training (NDRRMC Training Evaluation Report, 2023).
