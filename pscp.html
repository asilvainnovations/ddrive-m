2. Governance Structure

Continuity Management Team (CMT) roles and responsibilities:

Role Key Responsibilities
CMT Leader
(Head of Agency)
  • Declares PSCP activation and deactivation
  • Final decision-making authority during incidents
  • Primary liaison with national authorities
  • Approves emergency resource allocation
Operations Lead
  • Directs recovery of Tier 1 critical services
  • Manages personnel deployment at alternate sites
  • Implements manual workarounds during system outages
  • Reports recovery status to CMT Leader hourly
Logistics Lead
  • Secures and prepares alternate site facilities
  • Coordinates transport of personnel and equipment
  • Manages emergency supplies (food, water, medical)
  • Ensures physical security of assets and sites
Communications & IT Lead
  • Restores critical IT systems and communications
  • Manages internal notification systems (call trees)
  • Recovers vital records (digital and physical)
  • Provides technical support to all CMT functions
Note on Activation Levels:

Level 1 (Minor): Department-level response, no CMT activation
Level 2 (Moderate): Partial CMT activation, alternate site preparation
Level 3 (Major): Full CMT activation, immediate relocation to alternate site
