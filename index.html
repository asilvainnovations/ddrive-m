The 7 Risk and Resilience Intelligence Modules

End-to-end disaster resilience management system tailored for Philippine local government units

1

Detection

Empower disaster preparedness with multi-agency hazard monitoring integrated from PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, NOAH, and NDRRMC. Using Google Maps API and Zoom Earth Map Visualization, this module delivers dynamic 3D geospatial threat mapping with precise geolocation tagging. Enhanced by Faultfinder API and Flood Detection API, users can interact with real-time maps to uncover spatial risk concentrations and severity levels—transforming raw data into actionable intelligence.

2

Diagnosis

Build resilience with a comprehensive ISO 31000-aligned risk registry. This module leverages machine learning and AI-driven analytics to assess strategic, operational, financial, compliance, and environmental risks. Users can upload diverse data—drone footage, images, text—for automated vulnerability detection. Risks are classified by type and severity, continuously improving through historical data training, ensuring adaptability to emerging threats.

3

Response

Accelerate emergency response with an AI-augmented treatment canvas that integrates DTI, DILG, and LGU protocols. This module enables structured risk treatment and mitigation strategies, supported by a robust incident management system. Track incidents, assign tasks, and manage resources seamlessly to ensure coordinated, efficient disaster response across agencies and communities.

4

Integration

Measure and improve organizational and community resilience with the UNDRR 10 Essentials scorecard and SPARC Tool. This module provides progress tracking, resilience scoring, and year-over-year improvement monitoring, empowering organizations to benchmark disaster preparedness and continuously strengthen their risk management frameworks.

5

Validation

Enhance preparedness through scenario-based simulations for typhoons, earthquakes, and floods. Featuring a 3D interactive simulation game, users can practice Emergency Operations Center (EOC) procedures and continuity planning. With stakeholder feedback and community engagement tracking, this module transforms training into real-world readiness.

6

Enhancement

Generate AI-powered DRRM, Continuity, and Recovery plans aligned with RA 10121, ISO 22301, and DILG standards. This module includes compliance dashboards, resource allocation tools, and recovery tracking features. With templates based on the PSCP second edition, organizations can streamline business continuity planning, disaster recovery, and resource management—ensuring resilience beyond the crisis.

7

Monitoring

Stay ahead of risks with a real-time monitoring and communication hub. This module integrates team alerts, emergency protocols, and cross-functional coordination tools for seamless collaboration. Users can generate instant reports, share critical updates, and make joint decisions—ensuring timely, coordinated responses during risk assessments and disaster events.
